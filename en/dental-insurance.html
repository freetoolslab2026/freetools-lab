Is dental insurance worth it?
Dental insurance is usually most valuable when your preventive care is fully covered and you expect basic or major work, but annual maximums and waiting periods matter. If you only need cleanings, paying cash can be cheaper. If you need crowns, root canals or multiple fillings, a good plan may reduce your total annual cost.
