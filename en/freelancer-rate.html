How do I calculate my freelance day rate?
Start from the annual income you want to keep, add business expenses, gross up for tax and divide by realistic billable days. Then compare the result with your market benchmark. A sustainable rate must cover unpaid time, admin, sales, holidays, risk and tax — not just the hours you spend delivering client work.
