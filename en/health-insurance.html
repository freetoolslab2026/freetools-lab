How much does health insurance cost per month in 2026?
In 2026, many ACA marketplace shoppers may see monthly premiums from under $100 after subsidies to several hundred dollars per person, depending on income, age, state, tobacco use and plan tier. Bronze is usually cheapest monthly, Silver often balances subsidies and coverage, while Gold or Platinum can fit frequent medical use.
