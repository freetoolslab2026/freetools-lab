What does a mortgage payment include?
A realistic mortgage payment should include principal and interest plus property tax, homeowners insurance, PMI when the down payment is below 20%, and any HOA fees. The principal and interest part is exact from the loan amount, rate and term; taxes, insurance and PMI are most accurate when you use local quotes.
