How much will a personal loan cost?
A personal loan cost depends on the amount borrowed, APR, term and lender fees. A lower APR reduces interest, while a shorter term usually increases monthly payment but lowers total cost. Origination fees matter because they can reduce the cash you receive or increase the financed balance.
