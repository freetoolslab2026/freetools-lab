Is pet insurance worth it?
Pet insurance is most valuable when a covered accident or illness would be financially painful. If expected covered vet bills stay below the deductible and premium, self-insuring may be cheaper. If a single emergency would force debt or delayed care, the policy can be worth it even when the average-year math is close.
