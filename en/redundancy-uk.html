How much redundancy pay am I entitled to in the UK?
Statutory redundancy pay depends on age, complete years of service and weekly pay. Each full year aged under 22 counts as 0.5 week’s pay, each full year aged 22 to 40 counts as 1 week, and each full year aged 41 or over counts as 1.5 weeks. Weekly pay and total statutory pay are capped.
