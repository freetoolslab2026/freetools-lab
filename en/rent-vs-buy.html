Is it cheaper to rent or buy right now?
Buying tends to win when you stay long enough, lock in a reasonable mortgage, and the home appreciates after costs. Renting tends to win when rent is much cheaper, you move soon, or you can invest the down payment at a strong return. The break-even point depends heavily on local taxes, insurance, rent growth and selling costs.
