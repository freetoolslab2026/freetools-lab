Am I saving enough for retirement?
You are on track if your projected retirement savings plus Social Security can support your target monthly spending after inflation. The biggest drivers are savings rate, years until retirement, investment return, inflation and withdrawal rate. Use your official SSA estimate and realistic plan contribution limits for a better result.
