How do I calculate SaaS ROI?
SaaS ROI is calculated by subtracting total software cost from measurable benefits, then dividing by total cost. A serious business case should include implementation, training, admin time, adoption risk and whether time savings become actual output, lower headcount needs or revenue growth.
