How much of my salary do I take home?
Your take-home pay is gross salary minus pre-tax deductions, federal income tax, FICA, state tax and any post-tax deductions. The federal and FICA parts can be estimated reliably from 2026 rules, while state withholding is best treated as editable because credits, local taxes and payroll rules vary.
