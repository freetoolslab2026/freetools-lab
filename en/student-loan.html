What will my student loan payment be?
For a fixed plan, the payment depends on balance, interest rate and term. Income-driven plans depend mainly on AGI, family size and the poverty guideline. A lower monthly payment can protect cash flow, but it may increase total interest and extend repayment unless forgiveness or subsidies apply.

Frequently asked questions

The standard payment is a normal amortized loan payment using your balance, annual interest rate and repayment term, usually benchmarked against 10 years for federal loans.
IBR-style payments are estimated from discretionary income: AGI minus 150% of the poverty guideline for your family size and state. The calculator applies either 10% or 15% annually, divided by 12.
